Check platform, source, licensing, and account-action risk.

- Niche: local-first AI content systems for creators and small agencies
- Target: solo creators, small agencies, founders

- Tone: sharp, practical, professional, direct

- source policy
- publishing policy

## Outputs
- risk-review.md
- blocked-actions.md

## Allowed Tools

## Quality Checks

- risky modules disabled
- approval gate present

## Escalation Rules
