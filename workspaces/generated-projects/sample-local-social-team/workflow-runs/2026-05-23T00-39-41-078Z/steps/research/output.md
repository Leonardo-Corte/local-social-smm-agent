### Research Task Output Contract

#### Overview:
This contract outlines specific requirements and guidelines for all research tasks performed within this workspace. The primary focus is to ensure that any recommendations, analyses, or proposed changes are aligned with the goals and objectives of the project while minimizing risks.

#### Specific Requirements:

1. **Target Workspace Alignment**:
   - Ensure that all outputs are tailored specifically to the needs and context of this particular workspace.
   - Provide clear contextual references where necessary to avoid ambiguity.

2. **Assumptions and Risks**:
   - Identify and explicitly document any assumptions made during research or analysis.
   - Highlight potential risks associated with proposed recommendations or actions, including technical limitations, user behavior unpredictability, etc.

3. **Automation Restrictions**:
   - Do not recommend using tools or methods that automate direct messaging (DM), comments, likes, or follows on social media platforms without explicit human approval and ethical considerations.

4. **Publication Approval**:
   - Content must never be marked as ready to publish without receiving explicit approval from a designated reviewer or project manager.
   - Include clear instructions for review and feedback processes before content is finalized.

5. **Recommended Fixes Section**:
   - Add a dedicated section titled "Recommended Fixes" where you outline specific actionable items based on your findings.
   - Provide concrete, bullet-point recommendations that address identified issues or opportunities for improvement.

6. **Proposed Artifact Replacement (if applicable)**:
   - If proposing a complete replacement of an existing artifact within the workspace, include this proposed new content in a fenced code block titled "Proposed Artifact".
   - Ensure clarity and completeness of the proposed changes to facilitate easy review and approval.

7. **Next Steps for Approval or Regeneration**:
   - End each output with concrete next steps, clearly indicating whether the work requires human review/approval or should be regenerated based on further data or insights.
   - Include estimated timelines for subsequent stages (e.g., "This task will require a detailed review by [Name] within 2 business days.").

### Example Format:

```markdown
# Research Task Output

## Context and Findings
...

## Assumptions and Risks
- [Assumption 1]
- [Risk 1]

## Recommended Fixes
- Fix 1: [Action Item]
- Fix 2: [Action Item]

## Proposed Artifact (if applicable)
```
```markdown
# Updated Content Example

...
```

## Next Steps
- This task requires review by [Name] for final approval.
- Estimated review timeline: 3 business days.

---

By adhering to this contract, we ensure that all research outputs contribute effectively and ethically towards achieving the objectives of our workspace.
```
